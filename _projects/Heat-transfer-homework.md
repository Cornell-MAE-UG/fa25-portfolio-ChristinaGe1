For this portfolio assignment, I selected Question 1 from Problem Set 2 in MAE 3240. This problem analyzed heat transfer through a system with multiple thermal resistances and examined how adding insulation affects heat loss from a water heater. By modeling the system using a thermal resistance network, I was able to calculate the heat loss and determine how insulation changes the required power input.

Working through this problem helped me better understand how to translate a physical system into a simplified heat transfer model. Instead of treating the equations separately, I learned how conduction and convection resistances combine in series to determine the overall heat transfer rate. This type of modeling is important in real engineering applications such as insulation design, electronics cooling, and energy efficiency analysis, so this problem helped reinforce both the conceptual and practical side of heat transfer analysis.
